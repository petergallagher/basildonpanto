About

This years HCPT production will be Sleeping Beauty.

Seating is unreserved with a cash bar serving hot and cold drinks plus beer and wine for the grown ups. So please come early, have a drink and grab a seat.

Tickets

Tickets are available to be requested in advance with cash payment on the door at the beginning of the show. Please contact us to let us know how many tickets you would like, with numbers of adult, child or PA tickets, let us know which show you would like to attend and we will reserve your tickets for you and send back a confirmation email within 1 week. You can then pay on arrival for your tickets.

Although we won't charge for PA tickets or young children we ask you to let us know all of the tickets you will require as there is limited seating. If possible it would also be great if you could bring the correct money for your tickets.

If you have any accessibility needs please let us know in advance and we will do everything we can to accomodate you.

Adults

£11

Everyone over 16 we ask pays £11 for their ticket.

Children

£6/Free

If you're lucky enough to be under 7 and can sit on a bench at the front we won't charge you for your ticket. For 7 to 16 year olds it's £6.

PA

Free

If you're coming with someone who would otherwise be unable to attend without your support we won't charge you for a ticket.
